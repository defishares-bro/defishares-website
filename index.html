Protocol Policy

GOLD-centered smart asset pricing

DefiShares changes upstream BitShares smartcoin semantics for managed DFS-backed bitassets. GOLD is the protocol feed anchor, non-GOLD managed bitassets publish TARGET/GOLD, and the chain derives the effective TARGET/DFS current feed.

Witness publishes TARGET/GOLD
+
Chain-generated GOLD/DFS
=
Effective TARGET/DFS current_feed
